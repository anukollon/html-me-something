Top 5 Tourist Places to Visit in South India

South India is great tourist destination with lots of exquisite beaches, hills, backwaters, waterfalls, temples and historical places. There are so many great destinations in South India and its difficult to select only a few. Here are some of my personal choices:

  1. Mysore
  2. Alleppey
  3. Hampi
  4. Munnar
  5. Goa

Mysore

Mysore is a city in the state of Karnataka, India. This place is located at the foothills of Chamundi hills and it is famous for its majestic Mysore Palace. Mysore Palace is one of the grandest places in the country. After dusk, the palace is lit with nearly 100,000 lamps - which are real treat for your eyes. Besides Mysore palace, there is Bridavan Botanical Gardens and Karanji Lake which are must see places while you are in Mysore.

Alleppey

Referred to as the Venice of the East, Alleppey is located in the beautiful state of Kerala. It is famous for its house boats, boat races and beaches. This place is dotted with beautiful backwaters which have plenty of house boats to stay in. A visit to this place will leave behind the most mesmerizing and relaxing experience of all times.

Hampi

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in India located near Hospet town in the Karnataka state, India. There are around 1600 monuments in Hampi which are spread over 16 sq miles. This place is recognized as one of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The area is simply stunning and you will be in awe of the millions of boulders surrounding the area.

Munnar

Munnar is a hill station located in the Indian state of Kerala. This place is breaktakingly beautiful with large expanse of tea plantations, forests, wildlife, breathtaking mountains and valleys.

Goa

Goa is a state with coastlines stretching along the Arabian sea. This tourist destination is famous for its beaches, nightlife, luxurious resorts and world-heritage listed architecture..
