Top 5 tourist places to visit in South of India:

There are so many great destinations in South of India, that its difficult to select only a few. Here are some of the places

  1. Mysore
  2. Allappey
  3. Hampi
  4. Munnar
  5. Goa

Mysore

Mysore is a city in the southern part of the state of Karnataka, India.

Bangalore

Allappey

Referred to as the Venice of the East, Allappey is located in the beautiful state of Kerala. It is famous for its house boats, boat races and beaches. A visit to this place will leave behind the most mesmerizing and relaxing experience of all times.

Hampi

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in India located near Hospet town in the Karnataka state, India. There are around 1600 monuments in Hampi which are spread over 16 sq miles. The area is simply stunning and you will be in awe of the millions of boulders surrounding the area.

Munnar

Munnar is a hill station located in the Indian state of Kerala. This place is breaktakingly beautiful with large expanse of tea plantations, forests, wildlife, mountains and valleys.

Goa

Goa is a state with coastlines stretching along the Arabian sea. This tourist destination is famous for its beaches, nightlife, luxurious resorts and world-heritage listed architecture..
